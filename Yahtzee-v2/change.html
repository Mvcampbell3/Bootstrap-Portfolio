Rules of the Game

**Disclaimer**
This is not for commercial use. This is a project used for educational purposes.

This is Yahtzee. The goal of the game is to have the highest score. You get to roll 5 dice a total of three times. You can hold onto any die you like by pressing the hold button. After the 3 rolls, you must fill out a score on the scoresheet to the right. You can do so in any order you like, but you must fill in a score. The upper scores are just the total number of each number listed. Bonus Yahtzees are gained when you get more than one Yahtzee. The scoring for the lower portion is below. For more information on the rules, please go Here.

  • 3 of a kind: score is total of all dice
  • 4 of a kind: score is total of all dice
  • Full House: score is the total of all dice
  • Small Straight: 30pts
  • Large Straight: 40pts
  • Yahtzee: 50pts
  • Chance: score is the total of all dice
